Here are some fun things I've built outside of my day job:

Fertility Forecaster

This is a tool that I built that estimates your chances of having your desired number of children and allows you to see the cost of waiting an additional year or two and see how much IVF improves your chances. I built a statistical simulation model that is based on published medical research. In the product, users can input their age and health history to see a personalized estimate.

Signal

Signal is an end-to-end encrypted messaging app for iOS by Open Whisper Systems. I made open source contributions to the project and built out support for audio messages.
