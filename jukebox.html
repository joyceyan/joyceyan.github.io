JUKEBOX

Jukebox is an iOS app that makes collaborative music playing easier when there's only one set of speakers in the car or room that a phone can be plugged into. One person can start playing music from his/her phone, and others can vote on upcoming songs to alter the playlist according to what the crowd wants.

I worked in a team of three. I worked on both front-end and back-end, and was responsible for setting up user accounts, Facebook login, geo-location based searching, and creating most of the front-end design. Jukebox is not yet on the app store, and is currently in a closed beta.

Technologies Used: Objective-C, Parse
